Peliver -> Python Deliver

How To Use

Send your files easily in your local network. You don't need to look for flash memory to transfer data from your computer to your friend's computer. You both just need to connect to a wi-fi (you can use the hotspot of your smart phone) and then run the server on your computer. your friend can connect to your server with your IP address in network and BOOM. He can upload his file and you can recieve it in your side.

For more information go through the README file in the repository: https://github.com/alifele/Peliver
